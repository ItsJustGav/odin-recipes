Chicken Parmigiana

Description

This is the description

Ingredients

  • Chicken (Breast are used in recipe, but choice is yours)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Italian Mixed Herbs
  • Tomato Passata (Also known as Tomato Puree)
  • Garlic and Onion
  • White wine and Chicken stock/broth
  • Chilli flakes (red pepper flakes)
  • Flour
  • Garlic and Italian dried herbs
  • Panko Breakcrumbs
  • Parmesan

Steps

Part 1: Dry brine marinade for chicken

  1. Split chicken breast in half, forming thin slices of chicken.
  2. Sprinkle both sides with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  3. Set seasoned chicken to the side to brine for 30 minutes.

Part 2: Parmigiana Sauce

  1. Put a pot on the stove and add the tomato puree, garlic and onion, Italian mixed herbs, White wine and chicken broth, and the Chilli flakes.
  2. Let ingredients simmer, allowing sauce to thicken. The sauce should thick with a saucy consistency.

Part 3: Crumbing!

  1. Place chicken breast into tub of flour
  2. Dip lightly floured chicken breast into egg batter
  3. Submerge chicken breast into breadcrumbs, piling them up
  4. Breast are now ready to fry! (or bake)

Part 4: Frying (or baking)

  1. Chicken Parmigiana does not require deep frying.
  2. If frying, a shallow fry will do the trick
  3. The chicken takes 4 minutes max to cook through until golden.
  4. If chicken is not thourougly cooked, it will continue to cook in the oven
  5. For baking, bake at 390F for 15 minutes.

Part 5: Smother and Bake!

  1. Top with sauce, covering about 80% of the surface (leaving some crunchy exposed bits!). Optionally sprinke with fresh basil.
  2. Pile mounds of mozzarella on top, shredded melts more nicely than slices. Then sprinkle with parmesan and drizzle with olive oil.
  3. Bake for 15 minutes, just enough for the cheese to melt and the chicken gets some brown spots. Caution! Don't overbake as it will lead to dry chicken.
  4. Finally, finish it off with a fresh sprinkle of basil and enjoy!
